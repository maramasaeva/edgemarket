## 1. Where to List

### Tier 1 -- Best Fit (list on all three)

#### Flippa (flippa.com)
- **Why:** Largest marketplace, dedicated "Crypto & Blockchains" category, strong buyer traffic
- **Listing fee:** $29 (Entry tier, 60 days) or $49 (Boosted, 3 months) -- both under $10K tier
- **Success fee:** 10% of sale price
- **Escrow:** FlippaPay (1%) or Escrow.com (1.2%)
- **Total cost on a $2,000 sale:** ~$29 listing + $200 success fee + ~$24 escrow = ~$253
- **How to list:**
  1. Create account at flippa.com
  2. Click "Sell Now"
  3. Choose category: Crypto & Blockchains > Website
  4. Enter metrics (traffic, revenue, tech stack)
  5. Use the "Get a Free Valuation" tool first to benchmark
  6. Select Entry ($29) or Boosted ($49) listing
  7. Publish and respond to buyer messages in Deal Room

#### SideProjectors (sideprojectors.com)
- **Why:** Zero fees, popular with indie hackers, good for side-project-priced assets
- **Listing fee:** Free
- **Success fee:** None -- platform does not handle payment
- **Escrow:** None (handle payment directly or use Escrow.com yourself)
- **How to list:**
  1. Create account at sideprojectors.com
  2. Submit project with description, tech stack, screenshots, revenue data
  3. Include demo link and source code access details
  4. Wait for team review/approval
  5. Respond to buyer inquiries directly
- **Note:** No escrow = handle payment carefully (use PayPal Business or Escrow.com)

#### Little Exits (littleexits.com)
- **Why:** 25,000+ verified indie hacker buyers, built for exactly this type of project
- **Listing fee:** Check current pricing (varies)
- **How to list:**
  1. Create account at littleexits.com
  2. Use "Project Valuation" tool to set a price
  3. Create listing with metrics and screenshots
  4. Connect with verified buyers through secure messaging
  5. Use platform escrow for transfer

### Tier 2 -- Worth Trying

#### Transferslot (transferslot.com)
- **Listing fee:** Free for 2 weeks, then $20 for 2 more weeks (includes newsletter feature)
- **Good for:** Quick, low-cost exposure to a curated buyer community
- **How to list:** Click "Sell My Product" and fill in the form

#### 1Kprojects (1kprojects.com)
- **Listing fee:** Free to list; $19 one-time fee to read/respond to inquiries
- **Good for:** If you price at or below $1,000 for a quick exit
- **Note:** Targets abandoned/pre-revenue projects, lower price expectations

#### Indie Hackers (indiehackers.com)
- **No formal marketplace**, but post in the community forum
- **How:** Write a "Show IH: Selling my AI-built crypto toolkit" post
- **Free, good signal, engaged audience

### Tier 3 -- NOT Recommended for This Project

#### Empire Flippers
- **Minimum requirement:** $2,000/month net profit averaged over 12 months
- **Verdict:** EDGEMARKET does not meet this threshold. Skip.

#### Acquire.com
- **Better suited for:** SaaS with recurring revenue, startups with traction
- **Verdict:** Free to list but geared toward higher-value acquisitions. Worth a shot if you want maximum exposure, but expect less traction at this price point.

#### Shopify Exchange
- **Only for Shopify stores.** Not applicable.
